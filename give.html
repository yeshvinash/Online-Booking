You are looking for an extraordinary gift?

Choose fun, emotions, adrenalin and happiness to give to your friends! Indoor Skydiving (Bodyflying) can be done by everybody between 4 and 104 years! There is no previous knowledge...

All packages include:
  • A 20 minutes personal briefing by your instructor. Learn the basic positions and how to behave in a wind tunnel
  • We provide you with your equipment (suite. helmet. goggles and ear protection)
  • You will enter the wind tunnel as a group with other flyers (antechamber). You have the opportunity to watch other people fly and progress in between your flights (every group stays in the antechamber for about 30 minutes)
  • A video system inside the antechamber allows you to watch your progression right after your flight.
  • After exiting the wind tunnel you receive a personalized debriefing including a flight certificate. Further more we will give you information on your development to become a bodyflyer.
